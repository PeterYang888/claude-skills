## 二、《The Art of the Deal》（1987）核心內容

### 11條商業原則（✅ 高可信度）

| # | 原則 | 核心思想 |
|---|------|---------|
| 1 | Think Big（想大）| 初始要求永遠高於實際目標，給談判空間 |
| 2 | Prepare for the Worst | 保守行事，確保下限 |
| 3 | Have a Backup Plan | 多數交易失敗，靈活性是關鍵 |
| 4 | Know Your Audience | 依賴直覺而非調查 |
| 5 | Have a Bargaining Chip | 理解對方的需求和弱點 |
| 6 | Buy Good and Make It Better | 用行銷創造溢價 |
| 7 | Promote（推廣）| 自己當公關，媒體曝光比廣告更有效 |
| 8 | Fight If You're Right | 捍衛正確立場 |
| 9 | Have a Good Product | 行銷不能替代實質 |
| 10 | Keep Costs Low | 小額節省積累成大額 |
| 11 | Enjoy the Ride | 專注交易的樂趣 |

### 核心直接引用（✅ 高）

> "I like thinking big. I always have. To me it's very simple: if you're going to be thinking anyway, you might as well think big."

> "People want to believe that something is the biggest and the greatest and the most spectacular. I call it **truthful hyperbole**. It's an innocent form of exaggeration, and a very effective form of promotion."

> "I always go into a deal anticipating the worst. If you plan for the worst—if you can live with the worst—the good will always take care of itself."

> "The worst thing you can possibly do in a deal is seem desperate to make it. That makes the other guy smell blood, and then you're dead."

> "Money was never a big motivation for me, except as a way to keep score. The real excitement is playing the game."

> "I'm the first to admit that I am very competitive and that I'll do nearly anything within legal bounds to win."

> "Most people think small, because most people are afraid of success, afraid of making decisions, afraid of winning. And that gives people like me a great advantage."

## 五、標誌性演講

### 2015年6月16日 競選宣布演講（✅ 高）

**墨西哥段落原始語境**：
> "When Mexico sends its people, they're not sending their best. They're not sending you. They're not sending you. They're sending people that have lots of problems, and they're bringing those problems with us. They're bringing drugs. They're bringing crime. They're rapists. And some, I assume, are good people."

**「美國夢已死」宣告**：
> "The American Dream is dead—but if I win, I will bring it back bigger and better and stronger than ever before. Together we will Make America Great Again!"

---

### 2017年1月20日 就職演講（「American Carnage」）（✅ 高）

> "Mothers and children trapped in poverty in our inner cities; rusted-out factories scattered like tombstones across the landscape of our nation... This **American carnage** stops right here and stops right now."

> "For too long, a small group in our nation's capital has reaped the rewards of government while the people have borne the cost. Washington flourished—but the people did not share in its wealth."

> "From this moment on, it's going to be **America First**."

---

### 2021年1月6日 橢圓形廣場演講（✅ 高）

> "We fight like hell. And if you don't fight like hell, you're not going to have a country anymore."

> "I know that everyone here will soon be marching over to the Capitol building to **peacefully and patriotically** make your voices heard."

> "We will never give up. We will never concede... You don't concede when there's theft involved."

⚠️ **解讀爭議**：辯護方強調「peacefully and patriotically」；控方強調「fight like hell」。兩段話均真實存在於同一演講中。

## 六、Twitter / Truth Social 文本模式

### 數量統計（✅ 高）

| 指標 | 數值 |
|-----|------|
| 2009-2021推文總量 | ~57,000條 |
| 第一任期推文 | 25,000+ 條 |

| 第四年平均 | 每日34.8條 |

### 大寫詞彙使用（✅ 高）

- `FAKE NEWS`（157次記錄）
- `WITCH HUNT`（124次；2018年8月穆勒調查期間高達每週9次）

## 七、貿易保護主義的40年一致性

- **1987年**：刊登全版廣告批評美國外交政策：「Let's not let our great country be laughed at anymore」
- **1988年 Oprah 訪談**：「America is being ripped off...We're not going to have an America in 10 years if it keeps going like this」
- **2016-2020年**：第一任期關稅戰
- **2025年4月「解放日」**：「for decades, our country has been looted, pillaged, raped and plundered by nations near and far, both friend and foe alike」

**核心論點框架**（✅ 高）：
1. **貿易赤字 = 被搶劫**：「They're ripping us off」
2. **互惠原則**：「The key word is reciprocal. If they charge us, we charge them the same thing.」
3. **美國慷慨被濫用**：提供軍事保護但未獲等價回報
