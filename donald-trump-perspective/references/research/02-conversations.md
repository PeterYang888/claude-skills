# Donald Trump 訪談、對話與談話紀錄研究報告

**調研時間：** 2026-05-24

---

## 一、早期重要訪談（1980–2000年代）

### 1988年 Oprah Winfrey 訪談（✅ 高，錄影存檔）

> **關於競選總統：**「Probably not... But I do get tired of seeing the country get ripped off… I just don't think I have the inclination to do it. If it got so bad, I would never want to rule it out totally, because I really am tired of seeing what's happening with this country.」

> **關於贏的決心：**「I wouldn't go in to lose.」

> **關於盟友付費：**「Kuwait — they live like kings. And yet, they're not paying. We make it possible for them to sell their oil. Why aren't they paying us 25 percent of what they're making?」

> **關於美國外交：**「I'd make our allies pay their fair share. We're a debtor nation.」

📌 **歷史意義**：1988年的訪談幾乎完美預示了2025-2026年的實際政策——「盟友應付費」、「美國被ripped off」的主張在近40年間一字未改。

---

### 1999年 Larry King Live（✅ 高，CNN錄影）

> 「The polls have been unbelievable. So I am going to form a presidential exploratory committee.」

> **對外政策原則：**「If other countries aren't going to treat you fairly, Larry, I think that those countries should suffer the consequences.」

> **關於副總統人選：** Oprah Winfrey 是他的「first choice」

### 1999年 Meet the Press（✅ 高）

> **關於 Pat Buchanan：**「I guess he's an anti-Semite. He doesn't like the blacks, he doesn't like the gays.」（展示早期立場）

---

## 二、辯論表現

### 2015–2016年共和黨初選辯論（✅ 高）

> **對 Jeb Bush：**「I know you're trying to build up your energy, Jeb, but it's not working very well.」

「Low-Energy Jeb」、「Little Marco」均在辯論中正式使用，有錄影為證。

---

### 2016年總統辯論 vs. Hillary Clinton（✅ 高，官方文字記錄）

> **第二場辯論（2016年10月9日），Clinton 說「很慶幸 Trump 不掌管法律」時：**
> **「Because you would be in jail.」**

> **關於 Access Hollywood 錄音帶：**「That was locker room talk. I'm not proud of it. I am a person who has great respect for people, for my family, for the people of this country and certainly I am not proud of it.」

---

### 2020年總統辯論 vs. Joe Biden（✅ 高，Cleveland，2020年9月29日）

本場辯論因 Trump 持續打斷 Biden 而被廣泛評為美國史上最失控的總統辯論之一。Biden 說出「Will you shut up, man?」和「clown」。

---

### 2024年總統辯論 vs. Biden（CNN，2024年6月27日）（✅ 高）

> **關於經濟：**「I built the greatest economy in the history of our country before COVID-19 struck.」

> **當 Biden 在移民議題上失去思路：**「I really don't know what he said at the end of that sentence. I don't think he knows what he said either.」

📌 **NBC News 統計**：Trump 在此場辯論約發言40分鐘，其中約50%偏離主題，共進行106次人身攻擊。

---

### 2024年總統辯論 vs. Kamala Harris（ABC，2024年9月10日）（✅ 高）

> **關於移民（Springfield, Ohio）：**「You see what's happening with towns throughout the United States. They are taking over the towns. They're taking over buildings. They're going in violently.」

> **關於 Harris 政策計劃：**「She doesn't have a plan. She copied Biden's plan, and it's like four sentences like, 'Run Spot, Run.' She has no policy. In fact, I was going to send her a MAGA hat.」

## 三、已驗證的核心語錄

### 關於權力（✅ 高，Bob Woodward 2016年採訪錄音）

> **「Real power is, I don't even want to use the word, fear.」**
> — 2016年接受 Woodward 採訪，此語成為《Fear》書名由來

---

### 關於「I alone can fix it」（✅ 高）

> **「Nobody knows the system better than me, which is why I alone can fix it.」**
> — 2016年7月21日，共和黨全國大會提名演講（PolitiFact 核實）

---

### 關於自我評估（✅ 高，Twitter 截圖存檔）

> **「Actually, throughout my life, my two greatest assets have been mental stability and being, like, really smart... I think that would qualify as not smart, but genius... and a very stable genius at that!」**
> — 2018年1月6日 Twitter

> **「I know words. I have the best words.」**

---

### 關於媒體（✅ 高，Twitter 公開貼文）

> **「The FAKE NEWS media... is not my enemy, it is the enemy of the American People!」**

> **「I came up with a term. Fake news.」**

> **「Whenever you see the words 'sources say' in the fake news media, and they don't mention names it is very possible that those sources don't exist but are made up by fake news writers.」**

## 四、Bob Woodward 書籍記錄

### 《Fear》（2018）（✅ 中高，部分有錄音佐證，部分靠幕僚轉述）

| 語錄 | 說話者 | 可信度 |
|------|--------|--------|
| 「Real power is, I don't even want to use the word, fear.」| Trump（2016年採訪錄音）| ✅ 高 |
| 「Let's fucking kill him!」（關於阿薩德）| Trump（幕僚轉述）| ⚠️ 中 |
| 「a fifth or sixth grader」（形容 Trump 理解力）| Mattis 關於 Trump | ⚠️ 中（Mattis 稱為「fiction」）|
| 「A fucking liar」| Dowd 律師形容 Trump | ⚠️ 中 |

---

### 《Rage》（2020）（✅ 高，18段錄音採訪，共9小時，Trump 同意錄音）

> **關於新冠肺炎（2020年3月19日錄音）：**
> 「I wanted to always play it down. I still like playing it down... Because I don't want to create a panic.」

> **關於COVID嚴重性（2020年2月7日電話）：**
> 「This is deadly stuff.」
> （⚠️ 而兩天後他在 Twitter 公開比較 COVID 與流感，淡化危機）

> **關於憤怒：**
> 「I bring rage out. I do bring rage out. I always have... I don't know if that's an asset or a liability, but whatever it is, I do.」

---

### 《War》（2024）（⚠️ 中，Trump 拒絕受訪，均為第三方轉述）

主要指控（非 Trump 直接語錄）：
- Trump 在離任後與 Putin 通話多達七次
- Trump 曾送 COVID 檢測機器給 Putin
- Trump 在 Mar-a-Lago 要求助理離開，以便私下與 Putin 通話

Trump 發言人否認：「None of these made-up stories... are true」

## 五、2025–2026年第二任期重要訪談

### 2024年12月 TIME Person of the Year 訪談（✅ 高，11,345字官方記錄）

> 「I ran a flawless campaign. It was 72 Days of Fury.」

> **關於大規模驅逐移民：**「There might be new detention camps... Whatever it takes to get them out.」

> **關於烏克蘭政策：**「I disagree very vehemently with sending missiles hundreds of miles into Russia. Why are we doing that? We're just escalating this war and making it worse.」

---

### 2025年4月 TIME「100 Days」訪談（✅ 高）

> **關於是否服從法院命令：**「Sure, I believe in the court system.」

> **關於「法律之治」：**「I wouldn't agree with it 100%... We are a government where men are involved in the process of law, and ideally, you're going to have honest men like me.」

> **關於競選承諾的「誇大」：**「I said that figuratively, and I said that as an exaggeration, because to make a point.」

> **關於驅逐美國公民：**「I would love to do that if it were permissible by law.」

---
